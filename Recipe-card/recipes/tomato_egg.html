Tomato Egg

Description

The Spinach, Tomato, and Feta Egg White Omelette is a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, crafted to create a delightful and nourishing breakfast experience. This culinary creation begins with a base of fluffy, protein-packed egg whites, carefully whisked to perfection. The vibrant green spinach leaves, sautéed to a tender consistency, lend a refreshing earthiness to the omelette, while the juicy red tomatoes provide a burst of sweetness and acidity that complements the other ingredients flawlessly.

As the omelette cooks to a golden hue, the crumbled feta cheese is sprinkled generously, adding a creamy and slightly tangy element to each bite. The feta's salty notes harmonize with the natural sweetness of the tomatoes and the subtle bitterness of the spinach, creating a well-balanced medley of flavors. With each forkful, the tender yet substantial texture of the omelette, combined with the lusciousness of the melted feta, makes for a satisfying and wholesome meal that nourishes both the body and the soul. The Spinach, Tomato, and Feta Egg White Omelette is a true celebration of fresh ingredients and culinary finesse, elevating the breakfast experience to new heights.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 3 egg whites
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh spinach, or more to taste
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese

Steps

Step 1
Heat a skillet over medium heat and spray with cooking spray.
Step 2
Whisk egg whites with salt and pepper in a bowl. Pour egg mixture into the heated skillet and swirl until eggs cover entire bottom of skillet; cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Arrange spinach, tomatoes, and feta cheese in the middle of the eggs. Cook until edges of eggs begin to curl up, 2 to 3 more minutes. Loosen omelet from the skillet using a spatula and fold in half; continue cooking until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 more minutes.
